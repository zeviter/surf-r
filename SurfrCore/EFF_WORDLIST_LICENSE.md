# EFF Large Wordlist — attribution

`Sources/SurfrCore/Resources/eff_large_wordlist.txt` is the **EFF Large Wordlist for Passphrases**
(7776 words), created by the Electronic Frontier Foundation and used here for the Diceware passphrase
generator.

- Source: https://www.eff.org/dice (file: https://www.eff.org/files/2016/07/18/eff_large_wordlist.txt)
- Author: Joseph Bonneau / Electronic Frontier Foundation
- License: **Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 United States (CC BY 3.0 US)** —
  https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/us/

The file is bundled verbatim (original `DDDDD<TAB>word` format); surf-r loads only the word column.
No modifications were made to the words themselves. This attribution satisfies the CC-BY requirement.
